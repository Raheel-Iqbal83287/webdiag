Our Services

Web Design

We create modern, responsive websites that look great on all devices. Our design process focuses on user experience, accessibility, and performance from the ground up. We work with you to understand your brand, your audience, and your goals before creating a tailored solution that meets your specific needs and exceeds your expectations.

Development

Full-stack web development using the latest technologies and frameworks. We build robust, scalable applications with clean code and comprehensive testing. Our development team follows industry best practices for version control, continuous integration, and deployment automation to ensure reliable and maintainable codebases that stand the test of time.

Website Diagnostics

Our flagship service uses the Website Diagnostic Tool to perform comprehensive audits of your site across sixteen modules. We identify issues with HTML structure, sitemaps, CSS/JS integrity, navigation, SEO, security, performance, accessibility, image optimization, and more. After the audit, we provide a prioritized action plan with specific remediation steps and estimated effort for each fix.

Performance Optimization

Slow websites lose visitors and rank lower in search results. We analyze your site's loading performance and implement optimizations including image compression, code minification, caching strategies, and resource loading improvements to ensure fast page loads and a smooth user experience across all devices and network conditions.

Security Audits

Protect your website and your users with thorough security audits. We check for Content Security Policy configuration, clickjacking protection, safe handling of external resources, exposure of sensitive files, and compliance with security best practices. Our reports include clear recommendations for addressing each identified vulnerability with priority levels based on risk assessment.
