Pricing

Our pricing plans are designed to fit every need. We offer flexible options for individual developers, small teams, and enterprise organizations. Each plan includes a full comprehensive audit across all sixteen modules with detailed reports in multiple formats including JSON, Markdown, and HTML.

Starter Plan - $99

Perfect for individual developers and small websites. Includes a one-time audit of a single website with up to 50 pages. Reports are provided in JSON, Markdown, and HTML formats with prioritized issue lists and actionable suggestions for each finding. You also get access to our knowledge base and community forum.

Professional Plan - $249/month

Ideal for agencies and teams managing multiple client websites. Includes monthly audits for up to 5 websites with up to 200 pages each. Get automated scheduling, email notifications when issues are detected, and CSV export for reporting to stakeholders. Priority email support is included with response within 4 hours.

Enterprise Plan - Custom Pricing

For large organizations with complex requirements. Includes unlimited websites and pages, custom audit modules, API access for integration with your existing CI/CD pipeline, dedicated support with a named account manager, and SLA guarantees with 99.9% uptime. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific organizational needs.

All plans include free updates to the audit tool as new modules and checks are added. We continuously improve our detection algorithms to stay current with evolving web standards and best practices, ensuring your audits remain relevant and valuable over time. New features are released regularly based on user feedback and emerging web technologies. There are no long-term contracts required and you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.
